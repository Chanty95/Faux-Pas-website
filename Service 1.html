Service 1: Personal Counseling

About This Service

This service entails in person counceling from well educated psychologists with meeting schedules that can be completely adjusted to your personal situation. With the purchase of this subscription you can plan a session once a week with a maximum duration of 2 hours. In these sessions our experts will give you advice on how to resolve your perfectionism issues and how to effectively change your mindset.

  • Group sessions (45min): $14,99 per session (20% discount if you plan a new personal counseling appointment.)
  • Online counseling $9,99 per workshop (10% discount if you plan a group session or personal counseling session.)
